### Admin commands require `-a` prefix.
- **desc** `clear` | `list` | `<UserID>` | `@mention`  
    *Desc-admin: clear, change or add user descriptions.*  
- **purge** `<count>`  
    *Deletes a number of recent messages from the channel.*  
- **uptime**  
    *Displays how long the bot has been running.*  
- **refresh**  
    *Refreshes slash commands for the current server.*  
- **guilds**  
    *Lists all servers the bot is currently in.*
- **fquit** `<guild_id>`  
    *Force the bot to leave a specific server.*
- **shutdown**  
    *Shuts down the bot process.*
- **broadcast** `#channel` | `ChannelID` `message`  
    *Sends a message as the bot to any channel.*  
- **role list**  
    *Lists all roles and members in each role.*
